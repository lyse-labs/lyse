# Commercial License

Lyse is distributed under **AGPLv3 OR a commercial license**, at your option.
You only need a commercial license if AGPLv3 doesn't fit your use case
(typically: you want to embed Lyse in a closed-source product or offer it
as a hosted service without contributing your modifications back).

The AGPLv3 path is free for the vast majority of users — individuals,
open-source projects, internal company use, CI integration on
closed-source projects, etc.

## When you need a commercial license

You probably need a commercial license if **all** of the following apply:

- You want to ship Lyse (or a derivative work) **embedded** in a product
  you distribute to customers, **without** disclosing your modifications
  under AGPLv3.
- Or: you want to **offer Lyse as a network service** (SaaS) under your
  own brand, without disclosing your modifications under AGPLv3.

You **do not** need a commercial license to:

- Run `lyse audit` in your CI on a closed-source project.
- Use Lyse internally at your company.
- Integrate Lyse with an internal tool that stays inside your network.
- Contribute to Lyse itself.
