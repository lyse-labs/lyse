# Code of Conduct

This project adopts the [Contributor Covenant v2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/) as our community standard.

## Summary

We pledge to make participation in our community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

We commit to acting and interacting in ways that contribute to an open, welcoming, diverse, inclusive, and healthy community.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all community spaces (GitHub repositories, Discussions, issue trackers, pull requests) and also applies when an individual is officially representing the community in public spaces.

## Reporting

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported to the project maintainer at:

**contact@getlyse.com**

All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly. The project team is obligated to respect the privacy and security of the reporter.

## Enforcement

The full enforcement guidelines and ladder of consequences (Correction → Warning → Temporary Ban → Permanent Ban) are defined in the canonical Contributor Covenant document linked above.

Maintainers who do not follow or enforce the Code of Conduct in good faith may face temporary or permanent repercussions as determined by other members of the project's leadership.
